Return Policy

We at Panaya want you to be extremely happy with your purchase and we apologise if it is not up to your expectations.

For whatever reason that you are not satisfied with your purchase, we would be most happy to provide exchanges and returns for all items purchased from us if the following conditions are met :

  1. Returns or exchanges will only be accepted in the case of a manufacturing defect or dispatch of the incorrect items.
  2. If the item falls under the Special Prices section, it is not liable for a return.
  3. A gentle reminder that all our products are handwoven by our weavers and are extremely delicate. The fabrics in each saree are pure and hence should be handled with great care.Hence, we offer returns and exchanges only in extreme circumstances.
  4. Your request for return/exchange should be emailed to privacy@panaya.in within 24 hours of receiving your shipment with the attached images of the defect if any.
  5. If the fall /Pico is done, the product is not liable for exchange.
  6. The product cannot be returned if the blouse is detached.

Saree(s) can be exchanged within 24 hours from the date of delivery subject if the following conditions are met:

  • The saree which is to be returned should be unused, unwashed, unaltered and it should be in its original condition.
  • All the tags on the saree should be intact.
  • The original invoice must be produced at the time of the request for exchange.
  • For safety issues, packages from any courier other than Blue Dart will not be accepted.

The company reserves the right and full discretion to decide if the saree is in the condition for exchange or return. All such decisions taken by Panaya shall be final and binding
