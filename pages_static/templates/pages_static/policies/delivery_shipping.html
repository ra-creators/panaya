Delivery & Shopping

To make your shopping experience with us pleasant, Panaya prioritises a quick delivery of your product within the given time slot.

We take utmost care while handling and packaging the product to ensure it reaches you in the best condition.

Barring Sundays and Public holidays, we ship all through the week.

We ship through reputed courier agencies only, this ensures your order reaches you well in the promised time and in proper condition.

Our Shipping Policy

  1. We offer free shipping for domestic orders.For international orders, we offer free on orders above 50,000.
  2. All sarees that you see on the Panaya website are stocked in our own warehouses and are ready to ship.
  3. All our products carry the official logo of Panaya, to ensure authenticity.
  4. We dispatch our products within 2-3 working days.
  5. Once the order is confirmed, we require at least 5-7 business days to deliver in India. As for international orders, shipments may take 8-15 business days.

Special Note

Night curfews and certain restrictions have been imposed in certain cities, and this may lead to a slight delay in the delivery of the orders.

Our teams will try our best to send out all the orders as fast as possible

If there is a delay in dispatch due to any natural calamity, public holiday or any unforeseen circumstance, we shall not be liable to return or exchange the goods.

If there is a delay in dispatch due to the product being out of stock, we may contact you through e-mail: privacy@panaya.in .

Thereafter you can :

  1. Wait for the original product.
  2. Select an alternate product.
  3. Opt for a store credit or refund.
