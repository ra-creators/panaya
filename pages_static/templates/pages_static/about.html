What is so special about Banarasi Sarees?

Banarasi Silk (also known as Benarasi Silk) is a fine variant of Silk originating from the city of Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh, India. Saree woven from this fine silk, known as a Banarasi Silk Sarees extremely popular all over India and across the world.

The art and culture of providing Banarasi sarees with silks is very ancient. The continuations of passing down the culture of artistry of the Banarasi sarees from one generation to another have never stopped. Persian motifs were being mixed up with the design of Indian silk for creating this distinctive flowery pattern of Banarasi silk sarees, which are so typical in present days.
