OUR STORY

About us

With our timeless and splendid sarees handcrafted by the skilled artisans of the city of Banaras, Panaya, formerly known as Oriental textile, is an aristocratic and elite name in the world of Banarasi sarees. Originating from the holy city of Beneras, the rich cultural heritage of India and the piousness of the Benarasi sarees has been well preserved by Panaya through it's beautiful sarees. Panaya has a history as rich and phenomenal as it's sarees, and although the brand was originally encompassed only within the holy city, it had been capturing hearts with its exquisite designs all over the world. The royal collection of the Benarasi sarees from Panaya has made their way into the hearts of people with taste and an eye for beauty worldwide. The six-yards of royal ethnic piece, has left impressed even Her Majesty the Queen of England. It was one of the greatest moments of honour for Panaya when the Queen herself visited us and appreciated our sarees. Adding another feather to our cap, the famous pop singer Goldie Hawn chose our Banarasi Saree for Michael Douglas in New York City, which was truly a cherished moment for Panaya. Our sarees have also found their way in the wardrobe of the former First Lady of the United States Jacqueline Lee Kennedy Onassis, among the prominent names in royalty across the Indian subcontinent. Priceless moments like this motivate us to work even harder to keep the legacy of Panaya shining across continents, being a contemporary heirloom for today's connoisseurs. Venturing beyond its city, the brand is now all set to showcase its finery and traditional artistry at a three day pop up at Chennai's Silkworm Boutique.

In, the most amazing moment for us when Queen England visited and she appreciated our work. Which made us more proud and motivated to work.

What is so special about Banarasi Sarees?

Wearing a Banarasi saree evokes a deep sense of age-old connection with one's grandmother, mother, aunt and every other woman who understands and signifies the spirit of Banaras-simplicity, faith and elegance. Wearing a saree from Panaya is an immersion into the slow luxury of the finest artisan hand woven textile from Banaras.

Preserving the highest level of traditional artistry and handicrafts of Banaras, while also never losing the modern touch, is the vision of Panaya. At Panaya, you can get both classically traditional sarees as well as sarees with a modern twist, and a mix of both. But whatever you pick is, the essence remains steady- radiance, simplicity and rich cultural heritage. Each of our saree is handwoven over several months, benchmarking excellence in quality of materials and designs, which attempt to match historical masterpieces. These sarees are also easy to wear and very comfortable, making you feel completely at home when you drape it, bringing out the radiant charm in every woman. Each saree has a story to tell, as each Panaya product has been made by careful detailing by the hands of a trained, dedicated artisan with skills which are passed down through generations and generations beyond generations. Let's restore the timeless bond with these sarees from the timeless collection of Banarasi sarees from Panaya.

How do we make Banarasi Sarees for you?

The rich and royal collection of Banarasi Sarees produced by Panaya by the traditional weavers of Uttar Pradesh, with skills that can only be developed over several decades. The dedication and perfection poured into the making of this classic historic element is unparalleled, making the charisma and the royalty that the sarees have is unmatched by anything else.
